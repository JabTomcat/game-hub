LVL 30 — 75 · NIGHTMARE & HELL
▍ PRIORITIES
Keep pumping Lightning Sentry. Max it out, then pump Death Sentry for the corpse explosion. Max Fire Blast last for the synergy bonus to Lightning Sentry damage. You want all resistances at 75% cap before entering Nightmare and Hell — gear for resists aggressively.

▍ RESISTANCE REMINDER
Hell difficulty applies a -100% resistance penalty. You need 175% total resist from gear to cap at 75%. Prioritize this before attempting Hell. Use Fade (1 point) as a temporary fix while you gear up — it provides a huge resist bonus.
