▍ BUILD OVERVIEW
The Lightning Sentry Trapsin is one of the strongest and most beginner-friendly endgame builds in D2R. You lay traps, they do the work. Lightning Sentry chains lightning between enemies while Death Sentry cleans up with corpse explosion. Strong in almost every area of the game including Uber content with the right gear.

▍ FCR BREAKPOINTS
Faster Cast Rate affects how quickly you lay traps. The key breakpoints for Assassin are 102% FCR (recommended) and 65% FCR (minimum target). Build your gear around hitting 102% FCR for the best trap-laying speed.

▍ MERCENARY
Use an Act 2 Nightmare Offensive mercenary (Blessed Aim aura). Equip him with Infinity for Conviction aura to break lightning immunities, Fortitude armor for damage and resists, and Andariel's Visage or Guillaume's Face helm. Your Merc's Conviction aura is what makes this build work in Hell — without it, many enemies are immune to your lightning damage.
▍ BUDGET ALTERNATIVES
Can't afford BiS gear yet? These are solid alternatives while you farm: Smoke runeword armor (75% all resists), Rhyme shield (+25% all resists, CBF), Tal Rasha's belt for lightning resist, and a Crescent Moon weapon on your merc if you can't afford Infinity yet.
