At Level 71 you choose between two Hero Talent trees. Both are good — pick based on playstyle:

Soul Harvester — Spend Soul Shards aggressively. More dynamic gameplay with lots of spending. Great if you enjoy fast-paced rotations.

Hellcaller — Wither replaces Corruption and adds a second Shadowflame DoT. More methodical, burst-focused every minute with Malevolence. Pick this if you prefer a more deliberate pace.
ROTATION & BASICS
▍ CORE CONCEPT
▍ HOW AFFLICTION WORKS
Affliction revolves around maintaining damage over time effects on your targets while spending Soul Shards on powerful spells. Soul Shards are generated by your DoTs and by killing enemies, and spent on hard-hitting abilities. Never sit at 5 Shards — that is wasted potential. The basic loop is: apply DoTs → cast Haunt → spend Shards → refresh DoTs → repeat.
▍ SINGLE TARGET PRIORITY
  • 1Maintain Agony on the target at all times — refresh before it falls off
  • 2Maintain Corruption on the target at all times
  • 3Cast Haunt on cooldown — this amplifies all your DoTs
  • 4Cast Dark Harvest on cooldown — make sure at least one DoT is on each target first
  • 5Cast Unstable Affliction when at 4–5 Soul Shards to avoid capping
  • 6Cast Drain Soul as your filler while waiting on cooldowns
▍ MULTI-TARGET (3+ ENEMIES)
  • 1Apply Agony to all targets
  • 2Use Seed of Corruption as your main shard spender — it hits everything
  • 3Use Vile Taint if talented — applies Agony to all nearby targets instantly
  • 4Cast Dark Harvest after DoTs are applied to all targets
  • 5Use Nightfall procs (instant Shadow Bolt) immediately — free damage while moving
▍ KEY TIPS
▍ THINGS TO REMEMBER
Refresh Agony within the pandemic window — you can refresh it when it has 5 or fewer seconds remaining without losing any duration. Don't let it drop.

Drain Soul on dying enemies — if a target is about to die, switch to Drain Soul for an easy free Soul Shard.

React to Shard Instability procs — these give you a free Unstable Affliction cast. Use it immediately.

Summon Darkglare timing — before using this cooldown, make sure Agony has been refreshed so it lasts the full duration of the Darkglare window.

Life Tap — if you are running low on mana, Life Tap converts health to mana. Your Voidwalker and Drain Life keep your health topped up so this is usually safe to use.
